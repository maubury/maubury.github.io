Welcome

Bienvenue sur Virtually French, the French classes online

With Virtually French, you can study French from virtually anywhere. All you need is a computer and an internet connection.

We run our classes on Zoom, the classes are lively and fun, students get to speak in small groups. Depending on the level of the class, we may study texts, short videos, have animated debates and meaningful conversations, all in French of course !

You will feel you are virtually in France.

About Me

Bonjour ! Je m’appelle Marine.

I was born and raised in Brittany in France. After finishing a degree in English studies, I moved to London where I taught French for business in the City for 6 years. I moved to Australia 18 years ago and I have been teaching French to adults ever since, either privately or at the Centre of Continuing Education and Macquarie Community College.

I love teaching and seeing students progress. I try to make the classes fun, with lots of interactions. Some of my students have been in my classes for many many years.
